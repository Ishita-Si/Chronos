# OEM Manual (Excerpt) — Bearings and Vibration

## Bearing Life
Drive-end and non-drive-end bearings are rated for 25,000 running hours under
correct alignment. **Misalignment is the dominant cause of premature bearing
failure** and can reduce bearing life by 60–80%.

## Vibration Limits (manufacturer)
- Acceptable: < 4.5 mm/s RMS
- Unsatisfactory: 4.5–7.1 mm/s RMS
- Unacceptable: > 7.1 mm/s RMS — investigate alignment, balance and bearing
  condition before continued operation.

## Alignment
Cold alignment tolerance: 0.05 mm parallel and 0.05 mm angular. A dominant 1x
running-speed vibration peak is the characteristic signature of shaft
misalignment.

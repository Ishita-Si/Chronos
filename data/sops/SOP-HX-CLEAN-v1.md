# SOP-HX-CLEAN v1.0 — Heat Exchanger Fouling and Cleaning

## 1. Fouling Indicators
- Differential pressure rising above 0.8 bar.
- Widening approach temperature / falling outlet temperature.
- Quarterly thermography flagged as "degraded".

## 2. Response
1. Confirm with thermography and delta-P trend.
2. Raise corrective work order for chemical or mechanical cleaning.
3. After cleaning, verify delta-P restored below 0.55 bar.

## 3. Root Causes
Cooling-water-side biofilm fouling is the most common root cause on crude
preheat exchangers (e.g. HX-11). Review water-treatment dosing if recurrence
interval shortens.
